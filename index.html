Biography

I am currently a 1st-year Ph.D candidate at Computer Vision Research Group, School of Software, Beijing Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof.Shunli Zhang. I received my M.S. degree from School of Information Engineering, Capital Normal University, in 2024, and my B.S. degree from School of Information Science and Engineering, Hebei University of Science and Technology, in 2021. I am particularly interested in developing simple yet effiective solutions to problems in reality from deep novel insights.

Research Interest

   I work in the field of cumputer vision, with a specific fucous on image restoration and enhancement in low-level vision. I mainly explore learning-based methods for efficient and interpretable image super-resolution. Recently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Dynamic Feature Unsampling
  • Image Restoration
  • Image Super-Resolution
